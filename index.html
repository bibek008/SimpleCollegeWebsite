Ganeshmansingh Academy

We are a non-profitable school running under the governance of Ganeshmansingh Foundation, Jhiljhile,Jhapa. Affiliated to The Council for Technical and Vocational Training(CTEVT) Nepal and currently provide 3 years of Diploma level courses in Information Technology Engineering and Civil Engineering.


Courses offered

Diploma in Information Technology

Diploma in IT Engineering curriculum is designed to produce middle level skilled technician updated with latest skills of computer and information technology with a view to cope with the emerging technological change. Many people in the developed, developing and under developed countries have been given emphasis for the broader application of computer. Computer Engineering has been contributing the world for the overall development and creating job or employment opportunities in both public and private sectors.

Diploma in Civil Engineering

Civil Engineering is one of the prominent and popular disciplines within engineering. Many people in the developed countries, developing countries and under developed countries have given emphasis for the broader application of Civil Engineering. This field has been helping the world for the all-round physical infrastructure development and it has been creating wage and self employment opportunities both in public and private sectors.
